convinced Nothing

10122024


convinced Nothing means nothing
again but in a New Way in the sense
of Course one does not just hit upon
the Answer and all its resounding tumblings
that Don't really elucidate so much as
Add a Texture of Course they left out
the Back the Side wherever you end up
Finding Nothing to be the state of things
and on as the Dust rearranges into
new confusions
