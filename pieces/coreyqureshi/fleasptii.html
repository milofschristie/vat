Corey Qureshi

Fleas pt. ii


We have no pets
I am uninterested in the cottage industries
 spawned by Arca and NTS
Spawned from Jersey as all greatness does,
 i Laze in the dirty tub in memoriam
of a child Me damned to only standing showers
 i Crave a future, aspire, playing the game,
having no pets, running no marathons
 the Cult of youth displays yr veins, mine
half varicose. I think standing vs sitting is
 the thrust of my life’s work. And standers
sit in discomfort from
a) hours of opposite
b) poor seating. Standing is poverty
and shitting a luxury earned in
 consumption. I can’t get the
rules of this set up right. They Know.
Watching unaware comrades struggle at
 the rules and lash out our way instead
It’s quite repulsive
I, am, dirty,, with,, confusion
and Worthy of a lash
